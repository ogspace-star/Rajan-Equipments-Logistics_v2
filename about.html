Our Story

Driving Progress Through Innovation

At Rajan Cranes, we take pride in being one of the most trusted and reliable crane service providers in the industry. Since 1985, our journey began with a single crane and a vision to deliver top-notch lifting solutions.

Over the decades, we’ve grown into a powerhouse offering a modern fleet of well-maintained cranes capable of handling projects of any scale.

Why Choose Rajan Cranes?
  • Well-Maintained, Advanced Fleet
  • Highly Skilled Operators
  • 24/7 Support & Quick Deployment
  • Competitive Pricing
