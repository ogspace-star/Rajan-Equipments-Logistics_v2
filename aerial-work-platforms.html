Aerial Work Platforms

Aerial Work Platforms (AWPs), also known as boom lifts or man lifts, are used for lifting workers and equipment to elevated heights. They provide a safe and stable platform for maintenance, installation, and construction work at heights.

Key Specifications
  • Working Height: 6m – 40m (depending on model)
  • Types: Scissor lifts, Articulated boom lifts, Telescopic boom lifts, Trailer mounted lifts
  • Load Capacity: 200kg – 500kg
  • Mobility: Self-propelled or towable options
  • Applications: Building maintenance, electrical works, signage, construction, warehouses
